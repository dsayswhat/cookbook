Dutch Baby (German Pancake)

Source: https://example.com/test-pasta

Basic Test Pasta

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. Put butter in pie plate and stick in the oven to melt
  3. Mix flour and milk gradually until smooth
  4. Add eggs to milk and flour mixture until even smoother
  5. Remove pie plate from oven and drizzle batter in circles
  6. Bake for 15 minutes
  7. Cover with lemon and powdered sugar and eat
