Corn Casserole

Corn Casserole

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 1 can corn (drained)
  • 1 can cream corn
  • 1 box Jiffy Corn muffin mix
  • 8 ounce sour cream
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 stick butter or margarine, melted
  • 1 scoop mayonnaise

Instructions

  1. Mix all together and bake at 350Â°F for 30 minutes or until firm like a cake.

Notes

  • Recipe can be doubled using: 2 cans corn, 2 cans cream corn, 2 boxes Jiffy mix, 16 oz sour cream, 6 eggs, 2 sticks butter, and 2 scoops mayonnaise.
  • Original recipe by Chef Cynthia Pierce.
