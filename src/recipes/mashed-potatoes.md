## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients
- 5 lb bag russet potatoes
- 2 sticks butter
- 1 package cream cheese (8 oz)
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Fill a large pot with water and bring to a boil on high heat
2. Peel enough potatoes to fill whatever bowl you are using for serving
3. Cut potatoes into approximately 1-inch cubes
4. Add cut potatoes to the boiling water
5. Reduce heat to medium-high and cook until potatoes are soft enough to squish with a spoon (about 20 minutes)
6. While potatoes are cooking, open the butter and cream cheese packages and add them to a large mixing bowl
7. Strain the cooked potatoes in a colander to remove all water
8. Add the drained potatoes to the bowl with butter and cream cheese
9. Mix or mash until smooth and creamy
10. Add salt and pepper to taste and mix well

## Notes

- For extra creamy potatoes, let the butter and cream cheese come to room temperature before mixing
- Adjust the amount of butter and cream cheese to your preference for richness
- These mashed potatoes are perfect for holidays and family gatherings
