A fresh and delicious berry pie featuring a cooked berry glaze folded with whole fresh berries in a pre-baked pie shell. No oven needed for the filling! Recipe from the kitchen of Pat Currie Galey.

## Ingredients

### Filling
- 2 cups fresh berries (for mashing and cooking)
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 4 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 cups whole fresh berries (for folding in)

### Crust
- 1 baked pie shell (9-inch)

## Instructions

1. In a medium saucepan, mash 2 cups of berries with the sugar.

2. Add the cornstarch and mix well to combine.

3. Add the lemon juice.

4. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and becomes glossy.

5. Remove from heat and stir in the butter until melted.

6. Cool the mixture slightly (about 10-15 minutes).

7. Gently fold in the 3 cups of whole fresh berries.

8. Pour the berry mixture into the baked pie shell.

9. Refrigerate for at least 2-3 hours until completely set and chilled.

## Time Information

- PREP TIME: 15 minutes
- COOK TIME: 10 minutes
- CHILL TIME: 2-3 hours
- TOTAL TIME: 3 hours
- YIELD: Makes 1 (9-inch) pie, serves 8

## Notes

- This recipe works beautifully with strawberries, raspberries, blackberries, or blueberries
- You can also use a mixture of berries for a more complex flavor
- Make sure your pie shell is completely cooled before adding the berry filling
- The pie is best served the same day it's made for optimal texture
- Top with whipped cream just before serving for an extra special touch
- Store covered in the refrigerator for up to 2 days
