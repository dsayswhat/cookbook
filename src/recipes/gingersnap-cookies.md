Classic gingersnap cookies with warm spices and molasses. These cookies have a delightful snap and traditional gingerbread flavor. Recipe from Lorna & Bilson.

## Ingredients

### Wet Ingredients
- 1/3 cup shortening
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 1/2 cups molasses (Grandma's brand recommended)
- 1/2 cup tap water (cool)

### Dry Ingredients
- 6 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1 teaspoon ground cloves
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

### Other Ingredients
- 3 tablespoons water
- 2 teaspoons baking soda

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine the shortening, brown sugar, and molasses. Mix well.

2. Blend in 1/2 cup cool tap water.

3. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, salt, allspice, ginger, cloves, and cinnamon.

4. In a small bowl, dissolve the baking soda in 3 tablespoons of water.

5. Add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture alternately with the baking soda water. Mix until well combined.

6. Refrigerate the dough for 2-3 hours until firm.

7. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).

8. Flour your work surface, rolling pin, and hands well.

9. Roll dough to 1/2 inch thickness.

10. Cut with a 2 1/2 inch cookie cutter.

11. Place cookies on greased or parchment-lined baking sheets.

12. Bake for 15 to 18 minutes.

## Time Information

- PREP TIME: 20 minutes
- CHILL TIME: 2-3 hours
- BAKE TIME: 15-18 minutes
- TOTAL TIME: 3 hours 30 minutes
- YIELD: Makes approximately 3-4 dozen cookies

## Notes

- The dough needs to be well-chilled to work with properly - don't skip the refrigeration step
- Make sure to flour your surface and hands generously when rolling out the dough
- These cookies are perfect for decorating or enjoying plain
- Store in an airtight container for up to 2 weeks
