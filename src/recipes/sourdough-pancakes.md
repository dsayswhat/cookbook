## Ingredients

### Wet Ingredients
- 1 cup sourdough starter
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil

### Dry Ingredients
- 1 cup flour
- 1 Tbsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
