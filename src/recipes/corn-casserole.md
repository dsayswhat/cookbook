## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients
- 1 can corn (drained)
- 1 can cream corn
- 1 box Jiffy Corn muffin mix
- 8 ounce sour cream
- 3 eggs
- 1 stick butter or margarine, melted
- 1 scoop mayonnaise
